Review system (Sentimental analysis)

Q:Program to find whether the given review of a product is positive or negative.

Example : Sam has purchased a phone on flipkart. As the quality of the phone was really good, he posted a review saying “Awesome phone has excellent display”.

The program should scan the review and say whether the review was a positive or negative feedback .


Approach to solve the problem:

1.Declare around 10 variables each for good and bad words like,
	Good1=awesome
	Good2=excellent
	……
	bad1=bad
	bad2=worst
	….
2.Ask the review as input from user.

3.Divide the input into words and compare it with good and bad words, increment a counter variable for each match.

4.Display the answer.
